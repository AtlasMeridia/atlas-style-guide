# Stationery Aesthetic Direction

Visual direction for ATLAS Meridia letterhead, envelopes, and note cards.

## Format

- **Size**: Half-letter (5.5" Ã— 8.5")
- **Grid overlay**: Dotted grid, 0.25" spacing, light gray (#CCCCCC)
- **Grid specs**: 23 dots per row, 35 per column, 805 total dots

## Visual Language

### Motifs

- **Ginkgo leaves** - Delicate, curving, botanical line art
- **Wrens and small birds** - Arranged with floral elements
- **Chrysanthemums** - Isolated arrangements, ink-style rendering
- **Chinese ink-style illustrations** - Traditional brushwork aesthetic, sailboats, natural scenes

### Aesthetic Qualities

- Hand-drawn elegance over digital precision
- Black and white line art with photographic delicacy
- Botanical illustration style
- Minimal, refined, not busy
- Spot illustrations rather than full bleeds

### Reference Themes (Midjourney sessions)

Front page options explored:
- Bird and floral arrangements
- Corner floral embellishments
- Scrollwork elements
- Iris arrangements

Back page options explored:
- Ginkgo leaf arrangements
- Chinese ink-style drawings
- Traditional sailboat imagery

## Production Assets

Large design files (PSDs, AIs) are symlinked at `assets-local/stationery/`.

Key files:
- `letter - front.psd` - Front page master
- `letter - back.psd` - Back page master (includes grid)
- `envelope-ginko.psd` - Ginkgo-themed envelope
- `envelope-crow eye.psd` - Crow-themed envelope
- `dotted_grid_generator.py` - Utility for generating custom dot grids
